<p><p>The dental healing screw market features two primary types: titanium and zirconium. Titanium healing screws are widely used due to their strength, biocompatibility, and resistance to corrosion, making them suitable for various dental implant procedures. In contrast, zirconium healing screws offer aesthetic advantages, as they are tooth-colored and reduce the visibility of implants, appealing to patients concerned about aesthetics. Both types serve the essential function of promoting soft tissue healing around dental implants, ensuring the success of restorative procedures.</p></p>
