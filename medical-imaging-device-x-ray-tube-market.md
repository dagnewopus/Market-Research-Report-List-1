<p><strong>The Medical Imaging Device X-Ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li><li>Rotating anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical imaging device X-ray tube market comprises two primary types: stationary anode and rotating anode X-ray tubes. Stationary anode X-ray tubes consist of a fixed anode that generates X-rays when electrons collide with it, making them suitable for low-output applications. In contrast, rotating anode X-ray tubes have a rotating target that allows for higher heat dissipation and greater X-ray output, thus enabling more efficient imaging in demanding procedures. Each type caters to specific clinical needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
