<p><strong>The Slurry Pump Impeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Open Impeller"</li><li>"Semi-closed Impeller"</li><li>"Closed Impeller"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The slurry pump impeller market includes three main types: open, semi-closed, and closed impellers. Open impellers consist of blades mounted directly to a hub, allowing for easy passage of large solids, making them ideal for abrasive slurries. Semi-closed impellers feature a partial shroud, providing better efficiency and solid handling compared to open designs. Closed impellers have enclosed blades that enhance hydraulic performance and minimize recirculation, making them suitable for applications requiring high pressure and flow rates.</p></p>
