<p><p>The women's golf wedges market includes two primary types: pitching wedges and gap (or approach) wedges. Pitching wedges are designed for shorter, high-lofted shots, typically from about 100-125 yards, providing precision and control around the green. Gap wedges fill the distance gap between pitching and sand wedges, ideal for approach shots of around 80-100 yards, allowing for versatility in gameplay. Both types cater specifically to women's preferences in design, weight, and shaft length, enhancing performance and comfort on the course.</p></p>
