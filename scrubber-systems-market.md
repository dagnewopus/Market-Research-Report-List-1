<p><p>Scrubber systems are essential for controlling emissions in various industries, utilizing either wet or dry technology. Wet technology employs liquid solutions to capture and neutralize pollutants, effectively removing harmful gases and particulates. This method is often used in applications like power plants and chemical manufacturing. In contrast, dry technology utilizes solid materials, such as lime or sodium bicarbonate, to absorb and neutralize emissions, making it suitable for operations with space constraints or where water use is limited.</p></p>
