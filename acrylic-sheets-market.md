<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic sheets market primarily consists of two types: extruded and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced through an extrusion process, resulting in a uniform thickness and affordability, making them ideal for applications requiring cost efficiency. In contrast, cast acrylic sheets are created by pouring acrylic resin into molds, offering superior optical clarity and durability, making them suitable for high-end applications like signage and display cases. Both types cater to diverse industries, including retail, architecture, and automotive.</p></p>
