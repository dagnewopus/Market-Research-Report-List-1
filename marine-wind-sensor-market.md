<p><strong>The Marine Wind Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine wind sensor market can be categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their installation and measurement orientation. Horizontal wind sensors are designed to measure wind speed and direction at the same height as the measurement surface, making them ideal for applications like marine navigation and weather forecasting. In contrast, vertical wind sensors measure wind characteristics at varying heights, providing insights into wind profiles in marine environments, which is crucial for applications like offshore wind energy assessment.</p></p>
