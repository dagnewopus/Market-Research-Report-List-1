Hybrid IAM combines both cloud and on-premise components, allowing organizations to balance control and accessibility. On-Premise IAM resides entirely within an organization’s infrastructure, providing complete control and customization but requiring more resources for management. Each type caters to varying security needs, regulatory compliance, and operational preferences.</p></p>
