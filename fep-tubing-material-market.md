<p><p>The FEP tubing material market is primarily categorized into two types: heat shrinkable tubing and extruded tubing. Heat shrinkable tubing is designed to shrink in diameter when heated, creating a tight seal around components, offering protection from moisture and environmental factors. Extruded tubing, on the other hand, is produced through a continuous process, featuring uniform thickness and smooth surfaces, making it ideal for various applications, including insulation and chemical resistance. Both types are valued for their durability and versatility across industries.</p></p>
