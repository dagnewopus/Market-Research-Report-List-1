<p><p>The Rare Earth Rubber market consists of various types, primarily Neodymium Butadiene Rubber (NdBR) and Neodymium-Iron Rubber (NdIR). NdBR is known for its excellent resilience and abrasion resistance, making it ideal for automotive and industrial applications. NdIR, on the other hand, combines the properties of neodymium magnets with rubber, enhancing performance in specialized applications. Both types leverage rare earth elements, offering superior mechanical properties and durability, catering to industries that require high-performance materials.</p></p>
