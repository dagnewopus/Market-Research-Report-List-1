<p><p>Ester gum comprises various types used in diverse applications. Polymerized Rosins of Glycerol Ester (PRGE) are derived from natural rosin and provide stability and durability in coatings. Glycerol Esters of Wood Rosin (GEWR) offer enhanced solubility and adhesive properties, making them suitable for adhesives and inks. Penta Ester Gum is known for its flexibility and low viscosity, widely used in adhesives and sealants. Other formulations include specialty esters catering to niche markets, driving innovation in adhesives, coatings, and food applications.</p></p>
