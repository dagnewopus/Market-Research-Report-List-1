<p><p>The Metal-clad Cable market comprises three primary types: Steel Tape Armoured Cable, Steel Wire Armoured Cable, and Thick Steel Wire Armoring Cable. Steel Tape Armoured Cable features a flat steel tape for protection, making it lightweight and flexible, suitable for indoor applications. Steel Wire Armoured Cable utilizes steel wires for enhanced strength, ideal for medium to heavy-duty environments. Thick Steel Wire Armoring Cable offers maximum protection with robust steel wire armoring, designed for high-stress conditions, often used in industrial settings or outdoor installations.</p></p>
