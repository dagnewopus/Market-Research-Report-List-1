<p><strong>The Crane Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PUR</li><li>PVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crane cables market typically features two primary types: PUR (Polyurethane) and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride). PUR cables are known for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to abrasion, making them suitable for harsh industrial environments. They maintain performance in extreme temperatures and offer excellent tensile strength. In contrast, PVC cables are generally more economical and provide adequate protection against moisture and chemicals but are less flexible and durable compared to PUR. The choice between them depends on specific application needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
