<p><p>The In-Building Wireless Solutions market encompasses Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) and Small Cells, both essential for enhancing indoor connectivity. DAS involves a network of antennas distributed throughout a building to provide uniform coverage, ideal for large venues like stadiums and malls. Small Cells, on the other hand, are low-powered base stations designed to boost signal strength in specific areas, making them suitable for dense urban environments. Both solutions address the growing demand for reliable mobile connectivity indoors.</p></p>
