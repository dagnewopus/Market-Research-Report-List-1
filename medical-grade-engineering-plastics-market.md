<p><p>The medical grade engineering plastics market encompasses several key types, each with specific applications. Medical grade polypropylene (PP) is valued for its chemical resistance and lightweight properties, making it suitable for disposable medical devices. Medical grade polyether ether ketone (PEEK) is known for its high strength and thermal stability, ideal for implantable devices. Medical grade polyphenylsulfone (PPSU) offers excellent durability and can withstand autoclaving, suitable for surgical instruments. Other materials may include copolymers and blends tailored for unique medical applications.</p></p>
