<p><p>The Automatic Passenger Counting System (APCS) market is segmented into several types, including IR Beam, Thermal Imaging, Video-Based, and Others. IR Beam systems use infrared sensors to detect passenger movements as they enter or exit. Thermal Imaging systems capture temperature signatures, effectively counting passengers based on body heat. Video-Based systems utilize cameras and image processing to analyze passenger flow, while Others may include methods like LIDAR or ultrasonic sensors. Each type offers unique benefits based on accuracy, cost, and application suitability.</p></p>
