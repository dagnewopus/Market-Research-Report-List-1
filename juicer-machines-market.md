<p><strong>The Juicer Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Masticating</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Juicer machines are primarily categorized into three types: centrifugal, masticating, and others. Centrifugal juicers utilize high-speed blades to extract juice quickly, making them ideal for soft fruits and vegetables but often produce foam and heat. Masticating juicers operate at slower speeds, using a controlled process to crush and squeeze produce, yielding higher juice quality and nutrient retention. Other types include twin gear and hydraulic juicers, which offer unique extraction methods suited for specific applications or ingredients.</p></p>
