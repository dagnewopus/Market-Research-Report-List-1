<p><ul><li>Dead Axle</li><li>Live Axle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beam axle market consists primarily of two types: dead axle and live axle. A dead axle is a non-powered axle that supports the vehicle's weight and helps with stability, commonly found in trailers and certain heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, a live axle is a powered axle that drives the wheels, integral for traction and control in vehicles like cars and trucks. Both types play crucial roles in vehicle dynamics, influencing performance, handling, and load-bearing capabilities.</p></p>
