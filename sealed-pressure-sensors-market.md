<p><p>The sealed pressure sensors market is categorized into three main types based on pressure ranges. Low-pressure sealed pressure sensors typically measure pressures below 15 psi, ideal for applications like HVAC and food processing. Mid-pressure sealed sensors operate between 15 to 300 psi, commonly used in hydraulic systems. High-pressure sealed pressure sensors exceed 300 psi, suitable for demanding environments such as oil and gas industries. Each type serves specific applications, ensuring reliable pressure measurement in various environments.</p></p>
