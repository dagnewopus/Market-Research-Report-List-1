<p><p>The tankless water purifier market features two primary types: RO (Reverse Osmosis) water purifiers and UV (Ultraviolet) water purifiers. RO purifiers employ a membrane filtration process that effectively removes contaminants, dissolved solids, and impurities, providing safe drinking water. In contrast, UV purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to disinfect water by eliminating bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms, ensuring its safety. Both systems are designed to deliver immediate access to purified water without the need for storage tanks, making them efficient and space-saving solutions for households and businesses.</p></p>
