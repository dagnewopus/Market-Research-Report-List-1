<p><p>The Power Station Waste Heat Boiler market is primarily categorized into two types: horizontal and vertical configurations. Horizontal waste heat boilers are designed with their drums placed parallel to the ground, allowing for easier maintenance and efficient heat transfer, making them suitable for large industrial applications. In contrast, vertical waste heat boilers have their drums oriented upright, which saves floor space and promotes natural convection. Both designs are crucial for optimizing energy recovery and improving overall efficiency in power generation facilities.</p></p>
