<p><p>Rotary pan filters are essential in the separation process across various industries. The horizontal type features a rotating cylindrical drum mounted horizontally, promoting efficient filtration and easy discharge of solids. Conversely, the vertical type has a vertically oriented drum, allowing for effective gravity assistance during filtration, making it ideal for applications with higher solids content. Both types cater to diverse operational needs, influencing market dynamics as manufacturers seek suitable solutions for varying processing demands, efficiency, and automation levels.</p></p>
