<p><ul><li>Annual Seeds</li><li>Biennial Seeds</li><li>Perennial Seeds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flower seeds market is categorized into three main types based on the lifecycle of the plants. Annual seeds produce flowers in a single growing season and die afterward, making them suitable for quick blooms. Biennial seeds require two years to complete their lifecycle, flowering in the second year, offering a unique floral display. Perennial seeds generate plants that can live for multiple years, providing recurring blossoms and long-term garden presence. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences and gardening needs.</p></p>
