<p><ul><li>FPSO</li><li>Tension Leg Platform</li><li>SPAR</li><li>Barge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floating Production Systems (FPS) are marine facilities designed to extract and process hydrocarbons from offshore fields. Key market types include Floating Production Storage and Offloading (FPSO) units, which combine production and storage capabilities. Tension Leg Platforms (TLPs) are anchored to the seabed with tendons, providing stability in deep waters. Spar platforms are floating structures tethered to the ocean floor, ideal for deepwater conditions. Barge markets utilize converted barges for production, offering flexibility and lower costs in shallow waters.</p></p>
