<p><strong>The Cognitive Screening Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mini-Mental State Exam (MMSE)</li><li>Montreal Cognitive Assessment (MoCA)</li><li>Clinical Dementia Rating (CDR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cognitive screening tools market includes various assessments for cognitive impairment detection. The Mini-Mental State Exam (MMSE) provides a brief evaluation of cognitive function, primarily focused on orientation, memory, and attention. The Montreal Cognitive Assessment (MoCA) offers a more comprehensive assessment, addressing mild cognitive impairment through diverse tasks. The Clinical Dementia Rating (CDR) evaluates the severity of cognitive decline and functional abilities. Together, these tools are crucial for early diagnosis and management of cognitive disorders, influencing treatment decisions and care strategies.</p></p>
