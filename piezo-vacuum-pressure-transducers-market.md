<p><strong>The Piezo Vacuum Pressure Transducers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Cathode</li><li>Hot Cathode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezo vacuum pressure transducers are key devices in measuring vacuum levels, divided primarily into two types: cold cathode and hot cathode. Cold cathode transducers utilize a strong electric field to ionize gas molecules, providing measurement without filament heating, making them ideal for ultra-high vacuum applications. In contrast, hot cathode transducers rely on a heated filament to ionize gas, suitable for high vacuum environments. Both types offer precise pressure readings, with choice depending on the specific application requirements and vacuum levels involved.</p></p>
