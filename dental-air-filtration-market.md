<p><p>The dental air filtration market comprises various types of advanced filtration systems, primarily ULPA and HEPA. ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters capture 99.999% of particles larger than 0.1 microns, providing superior protection against finer aerosols and pathogens, making them ideal for dental practices. HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters are highly effective, capturing 99.97% of particles larger than 0.3 microns, ensuring a clean air environment. Both types are essential for maintaining safe and hygienic conditions in dental settings, enhancing patient and staff safety.</p></p>
