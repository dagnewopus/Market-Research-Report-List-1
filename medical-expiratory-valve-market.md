<p><p>The medical expiratory valve market is categorized into two main types: single-use and reusable valves. Single-use expiratory valves are designed for one-time applications, ensuring sterility and reducing the risk of cross-contamination, making them ideal for hospitals and emergency settings. In contrast, reusable valves are built for prolonged use and can be cleaned and sterilized between uses, offering cost-effectiveness for facilities managing high patient volumes. Each type serves distinct needs in respiratory management and patient safety.</p></p>
