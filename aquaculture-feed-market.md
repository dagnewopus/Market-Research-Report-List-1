<p><strong>The Aquaculture Feed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mash Feed</li><li>Pellet Feed</li><li>Expanded Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aquaculture feed market is divided into three main types: mash feed, pellet feed, and expanded feed. Mash feed consists of finely ground ingredients mixed together, making it suitable for small fish. Pellet feed is produced by compressing ingredients into small, uniform pellets, ideal for a wide range of aquatic species due to its ease of feeding and reduced waste. Expanded feed undergoes a cooking and extrusion process, resulting in a larger, buoyant product that enhances digestibility and nutrient absorption.</p></p>
