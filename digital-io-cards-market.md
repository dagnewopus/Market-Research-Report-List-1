<p><p>Digital I/O cards are essential for interfacing digital signals between computers and external devices, categorized mainly into four market types. Serial I/O cards utilize standard serial communication protocols for data exchange, while TTL cards operate with Transistor-Transistor Logic levels for faster communication in embedded systems. USB I/O cards offer plug-and-play connectivity, providing ease of use and versatility in modern applications. Ethernet I/O cards connect devices over local networks, enabling remote control and monitoring for industrial automation and IoT applications.</p></p>
