<p><strong>The Small Distribution Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pole-mounted Transformers</li><li>Pad-mounted Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small distribution transformers market includes pole-mounted and pad-mounted transformers, both essential for efficient electrical distribution. Pole-mounted transformers are installed on utility poles, ideal for rural and suburban areas, providing high voltage conversion while minimizing ground space usage. In contrast, pad-mounted transformers are installed on concrete pads at ground level, often used in residential and commercial settings where aesthetics and accessibility are important. Both types facilitate reliable power distribution while addressing space and installation requirements.</p></p>
