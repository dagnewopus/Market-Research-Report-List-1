<p><p>The traction wheels market is divided into two main types based on power supply: Direct Current (DC) and Alternating Current (AC). DC traction wheels typically provide high torque and are commonly used in applications like electric trams and locomotives, emphasizing simple control and efficiency at lower speeds. In contrast, AC traction wheels are known for their effectiveness in high-speed scenarios, offering better energy efficiency and performance. Each type caters to specific transportation needs, influencing design and operational requirements.</p></p>
