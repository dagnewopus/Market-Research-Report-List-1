<p><p>Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) management devices primarily include CSF shunts and CSF drainage systems. CSF shunts are used to divert excess fluid from the brain to another part of the body, alleviating conditions like hydrocephalus. CSF drainage systems, on the other hand, facilitate the controlled removal of cerebrospinal fluid to relieve pressure or monitor conditions. Both device types are crucial for managing neurological disorders, ensuring effective treatment and enhancing patient quality of life.</p></p>
