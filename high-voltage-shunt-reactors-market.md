Dry Type Reactors use solid insulation, making them suitable for indoor applications with minimal maintenance. Conversely, Oil-Immersed Reactors utilize insulating oil to enhance cooling and insulation, which is advantageous for high-capacity installations. Both types serve to absorb excess reactive power, improve system stability, and reduce losses in power transmission and distribution networks.</p></p>
