<p><p>The Ceramic Auxiliaries Market includes various types that enhance the performance of ceramic materials. Rheology modifiers adjust the flow and viscosity of slurries, ensuring optimal processing. Defoamers eliminate air bubbles in mixtures, improving product quality. Dispersants help evenly distribute ceramic particles, preventing agglomeration and ensuring uniform properties. Wetting agents improve the interaction between particles and liquids, enhancing dispersion and application. Other auxiliary products may include biocides or adhesion promoters, further optimizing ceramic formulations for various applications.</p></p>
