<p><strong>The Endotoxin Assay Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Clot Endotoxin Test</li><li>Chromogenic Endotoxin Test</li><li>Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endotoxin Assay Kits market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Gel Clot Endotoxin Test**: This method uses a gel formation to detect endotoxins and is primarily suitable for various applications in pharmaceuticals. </p><p>2. **Chromogenic Endotoxin Test**: This assay utilizes chromogenic substrates, producing a color change in response to endotoxin presence, enabling quantitative analysis. </p><p>3. **Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test**: This approach measures bacterial lysis-induced turbidity changes in a sample, facilitating rapid endotoxin quantification and is often favored for its sensitivity and efficiency.</p></p>
