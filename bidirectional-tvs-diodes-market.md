<p><p>Bidirectional TVS diodes, crucial for protecting electronic circuits from voltage spikes, are available in two primary types: axial and radial. Axial bidirectional TVS diodes feature a cylindrical shape with leads extending from either end, ideal for applications where space allows for linear mounting. In contrast, radial bidirectional TVS diodes have leads that extend from a single side, facilitating easier PCB placement in compact spaces. Both types serve similar protective functions but cater to different design requirements in electronic applications.</p></p>
