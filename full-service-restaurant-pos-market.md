<p><p>The Full Service Restaurant POS market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based POS systems operate on internet servers, allowing real-time data access, scalability, and reduced upfront costs, which appeal to many modern restaurants. In contrast, on-premise POS systems are installed locally on the restaurant's hardware, providing direct control and potentially enhanced security for sensitive data, but requiring higher initial investments and maintenance.
